Welcome to the Randall Reader Project Website!

The Randall Reader is currently just an idea. I am planning to create a portable text-file reader, using the Doubletalk text-to-speech voice popular toward the end of the last century.

The User's manual is a work in progress and outlines a final, aspirational design for the first version of the device. My development diary will chart the progress of the project, starting today with the first delivery of the necessary hardware to take this from an idea to a reality.
